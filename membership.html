Membership

A seasonal membership provides access to our seven outdoor courts and the use of our online booking service; there are no initiation fees or court fees for members. We offer a variety of programming to enable new members to meet players of a similar playing level: Challenge Ladders, Drop In Doubles sessions, Tennis Socials, Group Lessons, and Club Tournaments. Our friendly staff will be happy to suggest playing partners, or simply add your name to a "Looking for a Hit" list. Players at every level are welcome.

Members receive reduced rates for lessons and junior programs.

The club officially opens on the last Saturday in April, but nets are often up earlier, weather permitting. The club's office closes on the Thanksgiving weekend but nets remain up until the snow arrives.

Membership Types

Adult

Over 18 years of age at the time of membership purchase

Couple

Two adults in a relationship, both persons residing at the same municipal address, or One adult and one child of said adult, either under 18 years of age or a full-time student. Both persons must reside at the same municipal address.

Family

Parent(s) and their children, who are either under 18 years of age or full-time students, all residing at the same municipal address.

Student

Over 18 Years of Age and enrolled in school full-time at the time of membership purchase. Proof of student status may be requested.

Junior

Under 18 Years of Age at the time of membership purchase.
